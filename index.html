About Me

I am an engineering student at Savitribai Phule Pune University, Pune. I’m eager to learn, proactive, meticulous and I love nothing more than working with people, building amazing software and solving technical problems. My major areas of interest are Web Development, Android Development, Open Source, Data Structure and Algorithms. I have also been exploring lately Django framework, NodeJS. I have experience of programming in C, C++, Java, Python, HTML, CSS. Doing Competitive Programming. I am a patient learner and like to work in collaboration. I am Open Source enthusiast. I have good experience with Version Control System. I am actively contributing to Open Source Community since last 1+ year. I have contributed to many big organizations like FOSSASIA, Systers. I have good experience with Android Development. Two of my Android apps are available on Google Play Store and 3+ apps that I have created for learning the purpose, available on my GitHub account. I have experience working with Google SignIn, Google Maps and Places, Facebook, Twitter, Firebase SDK. I did my Summer 2016 internship at RacksonsIT, Jaysingpur working with C-Sharp MVC technology, Windows, and Web Forms. I'm available for remote work - if you would like to build something together!
